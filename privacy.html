Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 8, 2025

Welcome to the HRTech Tools Directory. This site was built to respect your privacy, load fast, and keep things simple. Here's how we handle your data.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

We do not collect, store, or track any personal or usage data. This is a static website with no backend and no analytics.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing behavior is logged
  • No data is transmitted to any server

2. No Cookies. No Trackers.

We do not use cookies, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, or any advertising scripts. This site runs entirely on the client side and contains no embedded tracking mechanisms.

3. No Input Forms

There are no forms, no logins, no contact fields, and no newsletter popups. You can use and browse this directory without sharing any information.

4. External Links

The directory includes links to third-party websites and tools. We do not control or monitor these sites. If you click on a link, you're subject to the privacy policy of that external provider.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we update how the site operates, this privacy policy will be updated and versioned here with the latest date.

6. Contact
