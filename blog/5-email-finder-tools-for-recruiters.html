5 Top Email & Contact Finder Tools Every Recruiter Should Know

Finding the right candidate is only half the battle. The other half? Getting their contact details. Whether you're sourcing passive talent or building outbound sequences, contact finders are indispensable in modern recruiting. Here are five top tools that make it easier to reach out — fast.

1. Apollo.io

Apollo is more than just a sales tool. With a massive database of verified emails and phone numbers, it’s become a favorite among recruiters. Its Chrome extension lets you find contacts directly from LinkedIn and enrich them with company info.

Best for: Large-scale outreach with verified emails
Free plan: Yes, with limited credits
Website: https://www.apollo.io

2. Lusha

Lusha’s clean interface and fast results make it a go-to for many talent teams. It excels in finding business emails and direct dials, especially for decision-makers. The Chrome extension works seamlessly on LinkedIn.

Best for: B2B prospecting and leadership hiring
Free plan: Yes, with limited monthly credits
Website: https://www.lusha.com

3. ContactOut

Built specifically for recruiters, ContactOut offers high email accuracy and a two-layer Chrome popup for ease of use. It’s popular for tech and healthcare hiring where email precision matters.

Best for: High-quality emails in tech and niche roles
Free plan: Yes, includes limited searches
Website: https://www.contactout.com

4. Hunter.io

Hunter is ideal for domain-based email discovery. You can search by company and get emails with confidence scores. It also verifies email deliverability, reducing bounce risk.

Best for: Domain searches and email verification
Free plan: Yes, with 25 monthly searches
Website: https://hunter.io

5. RocketReach

Known for its deep reach into both work and personal email databases, RocketReach combines contact accuracy with enrichment features. Useful for both recruiting and BD.

Best for: Personal + work email combinations
Free plan: Yes, with limited lookups
Website: https://rocketreach.co
